General Services Architecture
-Hierarchy of host locations
  -Region
    -These have city, state, or part of state names
  -Availability Zone
    -Multiple, isolated, and physically independent from each other within a region.
    -All connected in a relgion by redundant, high speed, & low latency network.
    -Availability Zone names are not shared between users or accounts.
      -Availability Zone names could be the same across users or accounts, but that is just by chance 
      -This allows for better use distribution across the availability zones
