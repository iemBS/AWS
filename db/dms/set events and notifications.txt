Notes:
  -SNS = Simple Notification Service
  -SNS provides notifications when an event occurs
  -Events are grouped into categories that can be subscribed to
  -You can create multiple subscriptions for different types of events
  -Event notifications can be sent by email or by SMS
  -Availability of SMS is dependent upon the region your DMS instance is in
