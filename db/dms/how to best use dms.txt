How to Best use DMS
Notes:

Main Success Scenario:
  1. Check on the number of tables you are migrating in parallel
  2. Reduce the load on your source database
  3. Disable backups and transaction logging
  4. Use a Multi-AZ deployment when performing ongoing replication
  5. Maximize resources available on the source
  6. Maximize available network throughput
  7. Maximize capacity of your replication server
  8. Reduce number of objects to be migrated
  9. Determine how to best handle the type and distribution of source data
