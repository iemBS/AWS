Main Success Scenario:
  1. Log into AWS site
  2. Go to Identity and Access Management (IAM) service
  3. On the left side bar, under "Access management", click on "Policies"
  4. Put policy name into "Search" textbox at the top of the list of policies and click "enter" on your keyboard
  5. In the "Policy name" column, click on the policy name link
  6. Read the JSON doc
  7. Click on the "Policy usage" tab
  8. Expand "Permissions boundaries"
  9. See the boundaries
