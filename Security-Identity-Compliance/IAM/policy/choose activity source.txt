Choose Activity Source
Note:
  -Activity source does not need to align with the principal entity that was granted access to perform the activity
  -A person or a role is a principal entity
  -A service can be granted access to perform activity. 
  -A role can be granted access to perform an activity. 
  -A role can never be an activity source.
Main Success Scenario:
  1. A service performs can activity when one or more conditions are met
