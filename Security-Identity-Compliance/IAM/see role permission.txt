  -These are actually permissions on a policy and the policy is associated to the role
Main Success Scenario:
  1. Log into AWS site
  2. Go to Identity and Access Management (IAM) service
  3. On the left side bar, under "Access management", click on "Roles"
  4. Put role name into "Search" textbox at the top of the list of roles and click "enter" on your keyboard
  5. In the "Role name" column, click on the role name link
  6. On "Permissions" tab, in "Policy name" column, click on a policy name link
  7. Read the JSON doc
